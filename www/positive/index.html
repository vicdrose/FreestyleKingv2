About this site.

The positive affirmation generator was created at the behest of a friend. After searching for positive affirmations online I realised how people may respond more positively to different forms of address in an affirmation and adjusted the site to reflect different pronouns, including the first and second person. I hope you find this site useful, please contact me if you notice any bugs.
The affirmations used here are originally from this site. Not all affirmations have been included in the generator.
